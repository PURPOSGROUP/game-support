FAQ
How do I install and start playing?

Download the game from the App Store, open it, tap Play, and you’re in.

Do you store my progress?

The game keeps a virtual balance only on your device. There is no cloud save and no account system. Deleting the app will remove your local progress.

Are there in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads?

No. There are no purchases, no subscriptions, and no advertising.

Is this an online game or multiplayer?

No. It’s an offline single-player experience. There is no matchmaking or online play.

What are the basic mechanics?

This is a casino simulator designed for casual play with virtual coins only. There is no real-money gambling and no cash-out.

Troubleshooting
First steps (most issues)

Force-quit the game and relaunch.

Restart your device.

Ensure you’re on the latest iOS and latest game version (check the App Store for updates).

If the issue persists, reinstall the game:

Press and hold the app icon → Remove App → Delete App, then reinstall from the App Store.

Warning: Reinstalling will reset your local virtual balance and progress.

Performance tips

Close other apps running in the background.

Ensure you have at least 1 GB of free storage on the device.

Disable Low Power Mode if performance is reduced.

Sending logs, screenshots, or video

Screenshots/recordings: Use iOS built-in tools (Power + Volume Up for screenshots, Control Centre for screen recording).

Email them to (cooper@purposgrouplimited.com) with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.
